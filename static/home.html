BrainBuddies is a social website that takes in user information manually and from the brain to match you with people who think similarly to you. Within BrainBuddies, you will be able to build your profile with preferences, watch videos to gather information, and connect with people!


Our Mission Statement

BrainBuddies is working to develop the next social platform that uses new technology to gather information from your brain. With this information, we strive to bring people together!


Our Vision Statement

BrainBuddies hopes to forge genuine connections between people using something that can’t lie, their brains. Our vision is to see a series of friendships and relationships formed through the simple aspect of thinking alike!
